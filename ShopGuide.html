Shop Guide!

Sweaty Betty

Sweaty by name, spangly by nature. SB is on the small side of Richmond's sporting emporia, but it's also perfectly formed, with friendly staff and a great selection of stock. Plus they run classes in store in evenings and at weekends...one even involved chocolate!

View details »

Lululemon

Richmond has one of only a handful of shops by this magnificent US brand, newly landed in the UK. Fresh designs and flatterig styles...just don't expect to go down a size! An excellent range of classes in the really very big store are another perk and the hunky instructors an added bonus.

View details »

Reebok

This isn't any ordinary Reebok store, this is a FitHub Reebok Store! With classes, competitions, and a number of hunky staff members this is a great, lower cost alternative to neighbouring Sweaty Betty and Lululemon. And the kit isn't half bad!

View details »

Wholefoods

A treasure trove of healthy foods, organic beauty products, and recycled homewares, this is one of our fave shops in Richmond. It may not be as cheap as Tesco, but the quality is excellent and there are some great value deals to be had

Sporting Feet
